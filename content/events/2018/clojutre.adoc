ClojuTRE is a Clojure conference organized by http://www.metosin.fi/[Metosin]. The event has single track, late start, short talks (20 minutes and 5 minutes Q&amp;A) and a funky after party for networking, discussions and craft beer. We welcome both newbies and seasoned Clojurists.
